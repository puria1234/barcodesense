Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 31, 2025

What We Collect

When you use BarcodeSense, we collect:

  • Your account information (email and password)
  • Barcodes you scan and product information
  • Basic usage data (how you use the app)

How We Use Your Data

We use your information to:

  • Provide barcode scanning and AI analysis
  • Save your scan history
  • Give you personalized recommendations
  • Improve our service

Third-Party Services

We use these services to run BarcodeSense:

  • Supabase - Stores your account and scan history
  • OpenRouter - Powers our AI features
  • Open Food Facts - Provides product information
  • Google - For sign-in (if you choose)

Data Security

We protect your data with encryption and secure authentication. Your data stays in the US and is not transferred internationally.

Your Rights

You can:

  • View your data anytime
  • Delete your account and all data
  • Update your information

Data Retention

We keep your data as long as your account is active. Delete your account anytime to remove all your data.

Children's Privacy

BarcodeSense is not for children under 13.

Changes

We may update this policy. Check this page for changes.
