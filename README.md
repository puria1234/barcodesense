## Barcode Scanning

BarcodeSense supports multiple ways to scan products:

1. **Camera Scan** (Recommended for mobile)
   - Click "Scan with Camera" button
   - Grant camera permissions when prompted
   - Point your camera at the barcode
   - The app will automatically detect and scan

2. **Upload Image**
   - Click "Upload Image" button
   - Select a photo from your gallery
   - The app will detect the barcode from the image

3. **Manual Entry**
   - Type or paste the barcode number
   - Click "Search" to look up the product

### Supported Barcode Formats
- EAN-13, EAN-8 (most common in retail)
- UPC-A, UPC-E (North America)
- Code 128, Code 39
- QR Codes
